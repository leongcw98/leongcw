About

Hi, I’m Leong Chee Weng, a passionate data enthusiast with a strong foundation in Marketing and Business Administration. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Marketing and a Master’s degree in Business Administration from Tunku Abdul Rahman University of Management and Technology (TAR UMT).

 

From a young age, I’ve been captivated by puzzles and problem-solving. To me, large data sets are like intricate puzzles, each piece contributing to a bigger picture. My excitement lies in uncovering patterns that others might overlook, transforming data into actionable insights.

 

In my current role as a Marketing and Sales Executive, I’ve leveraged my analytical skills to dissect and interpret sales and marketing data. This hands-on experience, combined with a deep-seated passion for data science, led me to pursue further education through prestigious online courses, including the IBM Data Analyst Professional Certificate, IBM Data Science Professional Certificate, and Google Advanced Data Analytics Professional Certificate.

 

Beyond the office, I dive into real-world data challenges, from case studies to Kaggle competitions, where I continuously push the boundaries of what data can reveal. Each project is an opportunity to blend my analytical expertise with creative problem-solving, driving me to explore new methodologies and insights.

Whether it’s optimizing marketing strategies or solving complex data problems, I am fueled by a relentless curiosity and a commitment to turning data into meaningful stories. I am excited about the endless possibilities that data science offers and look forward to connecting with like-minded professionals and exploring new opportunities.
